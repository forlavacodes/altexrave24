About the Event

What is Alte -X Rave?

  • Alte-X Rave is a cutting-edge Gen-Z Event that embodies creativity, style, and individuality. The term, Alte - X is derived from "Alternate" and "Expression," reflecting its innovative and unique nature. Initiated by a Nigerian group, The Altenate Gang (TAG), this movement aims to redefine and digitalize the creative industry, steering clear of the ordinary.
